Creamy Garlic Parmesan Chicken

PREP TIME: 45 MINUTES




Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley (chopped, for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Season chicken breasts with salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning.

  2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat and sear chicken until golden brown (about 5 minutes each side). Remove and set aside.

  3. In the same skillet, add minced garlic and cook for 1 minute.

  4. Pour in heavy cream and chicken broth. Stir and bring to a gentle simmer.

  5. Add Parmesan cheese and whisk until sauce thickens.

  6. Return chicken to skillet, spoon sauce over, and simmer for 5–7 minutes until chicken is fully cooked.

  7. Garnish with parsley and serve with pasta, rice, or vegetables.




Honey Garlic Salmon

PREP TIME: 50 MINUTES




Ingredients

  • 4 salmon fillets (about 6 oz each)
  • 3 tbsp honey
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp fresh lemon juice
  • 3 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil or butter
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley or green onions (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together honey, soy sauce, lemon juice, and garlic.

  2. Pat salmon fillets dry with a paper towel, then season lightly with salt and pepper.

  3. Heat olive oil or butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat.

  4. Place salmon fillets skin-side down and cook for 3–4 minutes.

  5. Flip salmon, pour honey-garlic mixture over, and cook another 3–4 minutes until salmon is cooked through and sauce thickens into a glaze.

  6. Spoon glaze over salmon and garnish with parsley or green onions.

  7. Serve with steamed rice and veggies for a complete meal.




Strawberries & Cream Parfait

PREP TIME: 20 MINUTES




Ingredients

  • 2 cups fresh strawberries (sliced)
  • 1 cup whipped cream (or vanilla yogurt for lighter version)
  • 1/2 cup granola or crushed cookies
  • 1 tbsp honey or sugar (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a glass or bowl, layer strawberries at the bottom.

  2. Add a spoonful of whipped cream (or yogurt).

  3. Sprinkle granola or crushed cookies on top.

  4. Repeat layers until glass is full.

  5. Drizzle with honey if desired and serve immediately.
